Welcome
D&J Brawdy Farms, Inc. is a wholesale grower of quality plant material and produce. Founded in 1999, the farm and greenhouses are located in the fertile Eden Valley region of Western New York.

Our Story

This family-owned farm and business began as a part-time operation in the mid-1990s producing baby lettuce for local retailers. In 1999, the farm became a full-time operation. Since then, the farm has grown into a thriving year-round operation composed of 160 acres, with nearly an acre of indoor production space.

Our focus both then and now is to provide the highest quality plants and produce coupled with exceptional customer service for our valued customers in an environmentally friendly and sustainable manner. In addition to being excellent stewards of our land, we strive to support our community as well. These activities include regular produce donations to local food banks, and employing recently resettled refugees from all over the world through partnerships with local agencies.

Food Safety & Sustainability

D&J Brawdy Farms, Inc. is focused on sustainability, minimal use of pesticides, and resource conservation in its approach to business. The farm utilizes Integrated Pest Management tools, professional scouting, and the most modern growing techniques such as trickle irrigation to ensure a sustainable operation.

The Farm is also certified by the United States Department of Agriculture and the New York State Division of Agriculture and Markets through third party audit as a Harmonized Good Agriculture Practices (GAPS) producer for all commodities. D&J Brawdy Farms, Inc., also is an advocate for farmland protection, and as a result, in partnership with New York State and the Western New York Land Conservancy, there is a permanent agricultural easement on our farm to ensure it remains forever green, and in agricultural production.
