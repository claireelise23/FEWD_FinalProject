Herb Production

D&J Brawdy Farms, Inc. produces quality potted herbs year-round for a variety of retailers, in a variety of containers based on customer specification. Items grown include basil, rosemary, thyme, oregano, dill, mint, chives, Italian parsley, cilantro, and sage. For sales information, please contact us.
