Vegetable Production

D&J Brawdy Farms, Inc. is an industry leading grower of grape and cherry tomatoes. Tomatoes are packaged to customer specification, and are available from late July through early October. All sales are arranged through James Desiderio, Inc. D&J Brawdy Farms also produces summer squash, cucumbers, peppers, and eggplant. All sales are arranged through Eden Valley Growers, Inc., and are available seasonally.
